** 模式匹配
*** 简单匹配
cl-case类似于switch
#+begin_src elisp
  (cl-case var
    (pattern1 (message "1"))
    (pattern2 (setq a "2")
    )
#+end_src
*** 强大匹配
pcase比cl-case更灵活,列表结构,变量,常量,字符串等
#+begin_src elisp
  (pcase var
    (pattern1 (message "1"))
    (pattern2 (setq a "2")
    )
#+end_src
